    From the data from Burning Glass Technologies (2018), the general skill the most sought for by employers is communication skills of 44367 employers. Communication skill is needed for IT professionals as it is required to communicate effectively with colleagues and supervisors in solving technology problems, it also helps with communicating ideas to teams(Comptia, 2020).

    Problem solving is the second highest demanded skill of 16445 selections. As IT continues to progress, there would be new problems that would need to be solved and the ideal jobs selected deals with clients most of the time, being able to solve their problems are the main importance as an IT personnel.

    Organisational skills is the third similar skill required for these jobs and it is selected by 15844 employers. Employers seek workers that are able to multitask and at the same time organise their work efficiently, this will help promote high performance and being able to fulfill tasks given (Comptia, 2020).

    Writing skill is the fourth most important skill set to have and it is wanted by 15590 employers. Email communication is also highly used by most jobs. Sending a good email is important as it will represent the company and building a good relationship with clients would positively impact the company.

    Teamwork is a huge part of most of the ideal jobs selected that is required by 14364 employers. Working with colleagues effectively and understanding their points of view will speed up finding solutions and finishing tasks better (Brooks, 2019). There would be less problems within the company if employees are able to work with each other.

    Planning skills are desired by 11315 employers and the last in ranking of importance is the skill of detail-oriented employees that is sought for by 8298 employers. In order to finish work on time, employees need to plan their work according to importance.
