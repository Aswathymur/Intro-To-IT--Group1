    Why we are here

    Due to the growing awareness of healthy eating and healthy living around the world, people are more conscious of their food intake. 75% of global respondents agree that they are what they eat and 80% of people prioritize food to prevent medical conditions or health issues (Nielson, 2015). Australiaâ€™s millennials are also conscious of their food choices and the biggest consumer group in healthy eating is 32% (Npd, 2018). Society has a growing concern of protecting nature and would like to lead sustainable lifestyles and having a platform to deeper understand the 7Rs of sustainability would be ideal for users to share their opinions and ideas.

    The app that is created is more interactive and allows users within the community to volunteer and help each other. There is a live stream feature in the app for members to show their cooking methods or allowing nutritionists to give a small talk for members that are interested in healthy eating. App consumers have been increasing more and more throughout the years and our project idea would have a high chance of a successful outcome. Apps that are available in the market are more focused on one feature instead of a compilation of different services given. Our app has multiple features like the smart fridge for members to easily check their food level and not to double buy the same fresh produce again. Members could give ideas on how to practice the 7Rs and suggest ideas to other members in their problems that they face.
