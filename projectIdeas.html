7RFoodCo - Mobile Application

Sustainability and Health

The vision of the app is to allow people to share their opinions and discussion or understanding of the 7Rs, healthy eating and sustainability. The 7Rs are rethink, refuse, reduce, reuse, repurpose, recycle and rot. There are multiple features of the app that includes recipes page, environmental friendly stores for fresh food and products, livestream platform and communal post bloggie section. The goals of the app includes:

- Increase understanding of the 7Rs and to share ideas with users

- Allow sharing of recipes for healthy living with tools such as an ingredient filter search

- Promoting sustainable food and product sourcing

- Allow users to enjoy live streams and promot inspiration to learn new tips on sustainability and recipes

- Act as a bloggie platform for users to share their ideas and create voluntary activities.

The benefits of the application are:

- Healthy community interactions between members

- Promotion of healthy eating

- Allows new users to understand sustainability and ways to practice the 7Rs

- Educates the community about benefits of sustainability

- Reduces excessive buying, by providing recipes for the available ingredients (Ingredient filter search), and also allows the user to update and store items that are stored in their pantry

- Wider reach to more people and easier accessibility to this app

- Provides users with simple tools that are quickly accessible on their smartphone

The target users are young adults and parents as they are more health conscious and place importance in a sustainable lifestyle.

Motivation:

Due to the growing awareness of healthy eating and healthy living around the world, people are more conscious of their food intake. 75% of global respondents agree that they are what they eat and 80% of people prioritize food to prevent medical conditions or health issues (Nielson, 2015). Australia’s millennials are also conscious of their food choices and the biggest consumer group in healthy eating is 32% (Npd, 2018). Society has a growing concern of protecting nature and would like to lead sustainable lifestyles and having a platform to deeper understand the 7Rs of sustainability would be ideal for users to share their opinions and ideas.

Features

Recipes:

The recipes section allows members to contribute recipes that are categorized according to any health requirements and eating preferences like diabetes, vegan, lactose-free but the recipes will be approved by a nutritionist beforehand. Users could have live streams on creating new recipes and their followers could choose what ingredients they want to try in the creative recipes live stream by voting option. Through the smart pantry, users will have options to cook different recipes using their raw ingredients.

Recipes album can be categorised into the following:

-Meal of the day (Breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, etc.)

-Countries/ethnic origin (eg. Mexican, Thai, Chinese, Korean, etc.)

-Dietary preferences/requirements: Vegan, vegetarian, pescetarian, etc. and allergens (nuts, eggs, dairy, etc.)

-Health requirements: diabetes, high cholesterol and many more

-Quick dishes

-“What can I make with leftovers?”

-Include amount of calories, nutrients contained in the meal

Environmental friendly stores for fresh food and products

Members of the app could source their fresh food through online stores that provide healthier fresh produce(organic), and bulk buy options to minimize plastic waste. Users have a smart pantry to list their available food at home so that they could avoid double buying. Members could buy daily use products that are degradable to reduce waste. Examples of products would be: metal straws, bamboo toothbrushes, freezer containers and food containers. Restaurants are welcomed to purchase products from these online stores as well.

The app provides a platform for users to either sell their unused products or to donate to people in need. Users could offer communal gardens location for people to throw their scraps for composting as well.

Live stream platform

This section allows users to have livestreams to have cooking shows or to have different livestream activities like sharing of ideas or have sustainability leaders to give a talk. Nutritionists could give talks on healthy eating and ways to minimize health problems. Users could scroll and choose which live stream that they want to watch and they are able to comment and interact with the live streamer.

User accounts, communal post bloggie

The communal post bloggie allows members to voice their ideas on their 7Rs lifestyle and this will provide guidance for people that are new to this sustainability lifestyle. There would be official articles from experienced people to provide an easy guide in starting the 7Rs lifestyle. Examples of articles could be composting, facts of growing plants, recycling and how to start a veggie patch. Users could propose voluntary activities like cleaning up their environment and allow communities of the same area to participate.

User accounts could do the following:

-Following profiles

-Section to post photos of attempted recipes Section to post photos of attempted recipes

-Categorization of their city/countries/location of residency

-Suggestions on ways to improve and to minimize wastage and it would create a healthy community in the app
