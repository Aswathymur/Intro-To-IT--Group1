Sustainability and Health

The vision of the app is to allow people to share their opinions and discussion or understanding of the 7Rs, healthy eating and sustainability. The 7Rs are rethink, refuse, reduce, reuse, repurpose, recycle and rot. There are multiple features of the app that includes recipes page, environmental friendly stores for fresh food and products, livestream platform and communal post bloggie section.
