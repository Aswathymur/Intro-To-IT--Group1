7RFoodCo - Mobile Application

Sustainability and Health

The vision of the app is to allow people to share their opinions and discussion or understanding of the 7Rs, healthy eating and sustainability. The 7Rs are rethink, refuse, reduce, reuse, repurpose, recycle and rot. There are multiple features of the app that includes recipes page, environmental friendly stores for fresh food and products, livestream platform and communal post bloggie section. The goals of the app includes:

- Increase understanding of the 7Rs and to share ideas with users

- Allow sharing of recipes for healthy living with tools such as an ingredient filter search

- Promoting sustainable food and product sourcing

- Allow users to enjoy live streams and promot inspiration to learn new tips on sustainability and recipes

- Act as a bloggie platform for users to share their ideas and create voluntary activities.

The benefits of the application are:

- Healthy community interactions between members

- Promotion of healthy eating

- Allows new users to understand sustainability and ways to practice the 7Rs

- Educates the community about benefits of sustainability

- Reduces excessive buying, by providing recipes for the available ingredients (Ingredient filter search), and also allows the user to update and store items that are stored in their pantry

- Wider reach to more people and easier accessibility to this app

- Provides users with simple tools that are quickly accessible on their smartphone
