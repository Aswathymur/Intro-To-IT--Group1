Overview

Here's our plan

The main outcomes of this project is to share the ideals of the 7Rs by creating an app that allows users to share cooking recipes that are both healthy and environmentally friendly through blogs or livestreams, as well as locating local stores for the users to obtain fresh produce. If the project becomes popular, the result would be a large variety of recipes in the form of a variety of blogs or a continuously growing list that can be filtered depending on what ingredients the user would like to use or what dish the user would like to make.

Not only would this spread knowledge of different kinds of cuisines, but it would also invite communication on individuals that actively share their opinions and methods, which could lead to self improvement. Furthermore, this would encourage a more healthy and sustainable approach to meals, prompting buy fresh products and reduce excessive buying that leads to food waste.
