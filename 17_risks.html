Risks

The risks we may face

We are building this app without having done any market research into the section. Because of this we run the risk of creating an app that our target users are not interested in. Investing time and money into this is a major risk for any sort of app development.

Another potential issue is our user base not taking our app seriously or the user base just not growing large enough and engaging enough on our platform. The way we are envisioning it is that it will hopefully grow through word of mouth and continue to grow.

Having said that though we will have to monitor the app closely and ensure that it doesn't outgrow our capabilities in terms of staff being able to monitor the app as well as the infrastructure that the app runs on. Server side bandwidth demand could be greater than our supply although this would hopefully be a fairly simple risk that we can sort out.

Given the nature of our app we would like to think that we would have strong ethics and morals and wouldn't be keen on “selling out”. Lucrative ad offerings could be made to us with questionable content and it would be important for our team to ensure that we don't fall into the trap of chasing money rather than our actual aim of sustainability and preserving nature.

Security breaches are always possible with any sort of site login. It will be necessary to have a strong security set up to ensure that we don't have any breaches of our user database that compromise.
