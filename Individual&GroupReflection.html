Individual and Group Reflection

Group Reflection

Even with a big group of seven, we were able to work together well without conflict. Group meetings and brainstorming sessions were consistent and each person completed their sections on time after each meeting. Throughout our many meetings, we were able to work together efficiently and there was good time management within the group to ensure that the assignment was constantly progressing. Each group member showed a good sense of responsibility in finishing their assignment sections selected before each meeting which helped us to finish the assignment on time. The brainstorming sessions for the project idea was good and members also gave their feedback on how it could be improved further.

The things that could be improved in our group is the feedback skills, even though there is less feedback or criticism given within the group, group members are more agreeable towards ideas, this helps the progress of the assignment to be completed fast and there was no argument that happened. The only weakness of the group is that there was no official leader that was nominated and instead there were a few members that were quick in providing ideas and direction for the team therefore it could be slightly time consuming when we have an issue but it had eventually been resolved through emailing to the tutor. What I have learned about this specific group is to not be afraid to ask questions as it is always better to ask if there is any lingering doubt and not leave it until the end of assignment. Not to mention that, this particular group is friendly and helpful which is always beneficial. There was never a need for us to delegate specific tasks for each person to contribute to the assignment but instead when someone asks for help, multiple members would volunteer themselves to help out.

For the github website, we were all contributing in solving any issue faced by members and would provide comments in improving each section. This assignment gave us incentive to figure out the requirements for our ideal jobs and we were able to keep in mind what we need to do to achieve our ideal jobs. Overall, even though our group is considered big and consists of 7 members, we were all very proactive and quick in response to help each other in to complete this assignment on time.
