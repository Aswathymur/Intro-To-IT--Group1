Scope and Limits

Scope

The vision of the app is to allow people to share their opinions and discussion or understanding of the 7Rs, healthy eating and sustainability. The goal of this project is to Increase understanding of the 7Rs and to share ideas with users, allow sharing of recipes for healthy living with tools such as an ingredient filter search, promoting sustainable food and product sourcing, allow users to enjoy live streams and promote inspiration to learn new tips on sustainability and recipes, and act as a bloggie platform for users to share their ideas and create voluntary activities.

This particular app will be done by using a UI and UX design application called Figma. Given the nature of our timeframe, knowledge and skills, we strive to make the app as best and functional as possible. Being that said, we will be able to show the core structure of the app such as the logo, the login page/sign up page, pantry includes examples such as vegetables, fruits, spices, meats and herbs, recipes with ingredients, steps and review for each recipes, environmental friendly stores for fresh food and products, Videos showing live stream platform and recordings and lastly, user accounts and communal post blogs.

The certain qualities that we will also strive for our project success is to make sure that our app developing procedure and method are up to standard, ensuring that there is no bugs or glitches by having multiple test runs on the app and having demo version for people to try out and report any bugs or glitches if there is any.

The time period for the start date was set at 28/09/2020 and the deadline of the completion of the app was set at 18 October 2020. In reality as we all know, in order to build an app from ground up takes time, knowledge and skills to think realistically, completing the app within the given time frame which is by the 18 of October may be quite challenging .

Limits/Constraints

As for all project ideas, they are bound to have certain limitations or constraints we have to work within and around it. Time, Cost, Quality, Risk and Resources are the main issues we may face during the project.

Due to time constraints, we are only able to produce and work within a certain amount of quality and work to this app to a certain extent. The cost for this will just be our own time as we are all using the free software to create this app, and due to the nature of using the free software, we are only allowed access to limited features but not the full features as full features require actual money. Being that said, the basic features are good enough for us to get this work done.

Finally, the resources are just from all of us in the group contributing our time, knowledge and effort into putting this project together without any support from other companies/investors/sponsors.
