Roles

Teamwork makes the dream work

In this case for our IT Project, we collectively as a group choose to not have any specific roles individually as we have discussed and agree that working together without individual roles bring greater benefits.

By working together without specific roles given, we as a group were able to accomplish tasks in greater efficiency, this means less time consuming and more effectively in getting the work done quicker, so that we can review it later on if needed.

In addition, since each individual only may have some degree of skills and limited knowledge, having more people and without individual roles means a more comprehensive range of skills can be shared, be it IT background skills, or business background skills, etc, this is beneficial as one may utilise their strength in certain areas to help out others and vice versa. When individuals use their strengths, it is often that the solutions are more effective compared to individual specific roles.

More people contributing also means we are able to brainstorm ideas and review as a group which idea is the ideal choice to go with instead of having just one idea to go with.

All in all, we decided to not have specific roles given to each individual as we believe that working as a group is far better, given the reason above.
