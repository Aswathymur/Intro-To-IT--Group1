Communication

Our group will communicate through a combination of face to face meetings (if possible), Microsoft Teams and Facebook. Face to face meetings are ideal for getting objectives clear for every member, making sure everyone is on the same page and the purposes of each task, as well as a vision of how everything combines into the final project.

Microsoft Teams is useful for smaller meetings, where everyone is informed of each otherâ€™s work progression, any small changes in the plan or stating any issues that may require possible assistance.

If a group member does not respond to communications, Facebook is an app that almost everyone uses and can be used to usually get a response from a member as it shows a notification on their mobile devices when a message is sent to them.
